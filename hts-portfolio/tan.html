Business Details

Tan WorkWear is redefining the uniform industry by blending fashion, comfort, and functionality. Specializing in corporate and healthcare workwear, the brand offers both ready-to-wear and custom-designed apparel that reflects each client’s identity. With a customer-centric approach, they aim to deliver modern, durable, and ethically crafted uniforms that inspire confidence and professionalism across industries.

How HalloTech Solution Helped

HalloTech Solutions worked closely with Tan WorkWear to build a seamless digital experience that matches the brand's focus on quality, style, and personalization. Our end-to-end involvement included:

  • Modern eCommerce Platform: Developed a clean, responsive website to showcase and sell their wide range of workwear products.
  • Catalog & Size Guide Integration: Embedded downloadable catalogues and dynamic size charts to simplify the buyer journey.
  • Custom Order Support: Built backend logic for handling custom order requests (MOQ logic), with future scalability for personalization options.
  • User-Friendly CMS: Implemented a CMS so the Tan WorkWear team can easily update product categories, stock, and promotions.
  • Performance & Security: Deployed the platform on AWS EC2 with SSL, optimized loading speed, and secure payment integration for safe transactions.
  • Future-Ready Architecture: The system is scalable for future feature additions like B2B login portals, live chat, and international shipping logic.
