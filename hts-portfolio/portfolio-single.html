Project Details

Chippy Cabinets is a Melbourne-based custom cabinetry and joinery business serving homeowners, builders, and commercial clients. Their focus is on crafting long-lasting, space-optimizing cabinetry with a high standard of craftsmanship and reliable service.

Business Overview

Chippy Cabinets delivers bespoke cabinetry solutions—ranging from kitchens and wardrobes to caravans and office installations. Known for their smarter storage design, durable craftsmanship, and honest project execution, they are trusted by clients across Victoria.
