HTS Freelancing Policy

At HalloTech Solutions (HTS), we aim to empower freelancers by providing high-quality client exposure and structured project management under our brand.

  • All freelancers working via HTS must disclose their projects to HTS for profile showcasing.
  • HTS reserves the right to feature selected freelancer work in client portfolios, marketing assets, or client showcases.
  • HTS will charge a standard 30% commission on the total project cost, ensuring platform operation, lead generation, and quality control.
  • Payments are processed only upon client approval and final handover. Disputes will be handled via our support channel within 3 business days.
  • Freelancers must maintain professional standards, timely delivery, and transparent communication throughout the project cycle.
  • HTS reserves the right to blacklist freelancers violating trust, misrepresenting services, or bypassing the platform after getting leads.

By continuing to work with HTS, you agree to these terms and commit to delivering client-first outcomes.
